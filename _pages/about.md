Hi! I'm a recent graduate of Barnard College of Columbia University, completing my Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Human Rights in 2026. After four years in NYC, I'll be [heading to Trieste, Italy](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/emma-burris-bbab3821b_belated-life-update-but-a-few-things-to-activity-7473916666037338112-e0gJ?utm_source=social_share_send&utm_medium=member_desktop_web&rcm=ACoAADeE474BBFGAatKk9w6e8Pj58n2ET_qsPro) this September to begin a Fulbright grant studying the Trieste model of mental healthcare. 

I'm passionate about rights-based, non-carceral crisis mental healthcare, especially that which takes place outside of inpatient psychiatric facilities and in community-based settings. I completed my senior thesis on experiences and outcomes of adolescent psychiatric hospitalization. I look forward to doing independent research in Trieste, famous for its psychiatric hospital closure and place in the deinstitutionalization movement. 

Reach out to me here or at [emmacburris (at) gmail (dot) com](mailto:emmacburris@gmail.com) if you'd like to chat!
